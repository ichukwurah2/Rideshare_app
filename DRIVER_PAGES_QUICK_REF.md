# RideFlow Driver Pages - Quick Reference\n\n## 🎯 Pages Overview\n\n### 1. DriverDashboard (`/driver/dashboard`)\n**Purpose**: Home dashboard showing driver availability and current ride status\n\n**Key Features**:\n- Online/Offline toggle button\n- Active ride display with rider info\n- 4 performance stats (rides, earnings, rating, acceptance rate)\n- Recent earnings list\n- Quick action buttons\n\n**File**: `src/pages/DriverDashboard.tsx`\n\n---\n\n### 2. PendingRides (`/driver/pending-rides`)\n**Purpose**: Shows available ride requests with acceptance timer\n\n**Key Features**:\n- 4 pending ride requests\n- 30-second acceptance timer per ride\n- Filter by rider rating\n- Sort by fare, distance, or rating\n- Accept/Decline buttons\n- Shows driver earnings, not rider fare\n\n**File**: `src/pages/PendingRides.tsx`\n\n---\n\n### 3. DriverHistory (`/driver/history`)\n**Purpose**: Browse completed and cancelled rides with analytics\n\n**Key Features**:\n- 12 ride records with full details\n- Search by rider name/location\n- Filter by status (All/Completed/Cancelled)\n- Sort options (Recent/Earnings/Rating)\n- Pagination (5 per page)\n- Performance stats header\n\n**File**: `src/pages/DriverHistory.tsx`\n\n---\n\n### 4. DriverProfile (`/driver/profile`)\n**Purpose**: Manage driver information and vehicle details\n\n**Sections**:\n1. **Personal Information**\n   - First/Last Name, Email, Phone\n\n2. **License Information**\n   - License Number, Expiry Date\n\n3. **Vehicle Information**\n   - Make, Model, Year, Color, License Plate\n\n4. **Bank Information**\n   - Account number and bank name\n\n5. **Availability Toggle** (in sidebar)\n   - Online/Offline status\n\n**File**: `src/pages/DriverProfile.tsx`\n\n---\n\n## 📊 Component Reuse\n\n### StatsWidget\nUsed in:
